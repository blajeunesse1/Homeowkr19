About Me>

Back when I finished my undergrad in 2012, I began a life of travel and freelance work, picking up9-5’s from time to time as needed. I began practicing graphic design work and decided I wanted to make a career out of it, that’s when I found UX design. As I transition into this field I take with my eight years of experience in many creative areas, as well as a fascination for people and understanding them. I enjoy exploring the mind through color and composition. I’m so excited to start my career in UX and see what surprises the future holds!
